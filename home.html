How to do your eyebrows like a pro

Fool everyone into believing its professional

Everyone knows the struggle in having uneven choppy unmaintained or even non existent brows, and everyone knows its the worst feeling ever, but through this step by step tutorial, i will help you transform your caterpiller brows into beautiful brows. Like this-

first lets identify the frayed folicals... For instance, for this persons brows, its too long with no straight line. This makes the brows look un-neat. To fix this we use the razor and keep it horizontal against the brows, making sure theres a fixed line. (We suggest you keep you natural brows as thick as possible). Once its horrizontal press against the brow, this cuts the follicals, giving you the perfect straight line needed to start. Additionally if you can see hair folicals above the cut line, you should use the razor in an angle and shave off the excess hair folicals up until the straight line.

once you have the perfect line, you should use the mini electric shaver and use it at the edges of your brows to give you a perfect crisp ending. Try creating an angled V shape at the edge. you can either do this using the razor, but id reccomend to do it with the electric shaver.

Finally the last and actually optional step. If you dont have a unibrow, then omit this step, however for the people who do use the big electric shaver for an effortless clean brow.
